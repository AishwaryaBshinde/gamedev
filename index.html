About Us

At Blue Black Teams, we’re a passionate group of game developers dedicated to creating unique, immersive experiences for players. With a focus on creativity, innovation, and cutting-edge technology, we aim to push the limits of gaming and deliver unforgettable adventures. Join us as we redefine what’s possible, one game at a time.

Our Projects

The Killer

The Killer is an intense, fast-paced action game where players engage in high-stakes combat, using strategy and skill to outlast opponents. With immersive gameplay, stunning visuals, and thrilling challenges, every moment is a fight for survival. Are you ready to claim victory?

Shadow Fighter

Shadow Fighter is an action-packed martial arts game where players take on the role of a skilled warrior battling through intense shadowy foes. With fluid combat mechanics, unique abilities, and challenging enemies, only the strongest will survive in the shadows. Are you ready to fight for honor?

Do or Die

Do or Die is a heart-pounding action game where every decision counts. In a world full of danger, players must navigate through intense challenges, face ruthless enemies, and make split-second choices to survive. It’s a fight for your life – in this game, you either conquer or perish.

Zombie Survival

Zombie Survival is a thrilling, action-packed game where players must fight for their lives in a world overrun by the undead. Scavenge for resources, build defenses, and battle relentless zombies in a desperate struggle to survive. Can you outlast the apocalypse, or will you become another victim of the horde?
